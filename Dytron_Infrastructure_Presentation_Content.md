## Project Execution
We usually take up any Project under the supervision of experienced & qualified Professionals in the fields of:
* Specialized foundation work
* Residential and mixed housing
* Social infrastructures involving heavy earth work, civil work (both sub structure and super structure)
* Structural steel fabrication and erection work
* Finishing and electro-mechanical work in all respects

### Key Practices
* **EPC Contracts:** We undertake projects on an EPC contract basis.
* **Modern Techniques:** Major activities like Shuttering work (Steel, Plyboard, wooden materials), pouring of Concrete, reinforcement, and other construction materials will be carried out with the help of the latest construction techniques.
* **Scheduling:** Entire equipment & Manpower will be deployed conforming to an approved work program (CPM/BAR Chart).
* **Quality Assurance:** A strict quality assurance plan will be followed.
* **Client Interaction:** Proper interaction from inception with the Client and their designated Architect/Consultant will be made at every step to resolve any onsite techno-commercial issues.
* **Safety & First Aid:** Proper safety measures and First-Aid facilities will be strictly taken care of at the project site.
* **Labor Strength:** We deploy an average of 100 to 125 labor strength at a time on a mixing basis (preferably from West Bengal and Assam) to keep the progress on schedule.
